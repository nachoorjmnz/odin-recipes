How to do Mayonaise

Mayonaise is a very popular sauce all over the world used to compliment too many dishes and it's very easy to prepare.

  • olive oil
  • egg
  • lemon
  • salt

Instructions

  1. Put the egg in a bowl and add a pinch of salt.
  2. Start mixing with a blender and add the olive oil little by little.
  3. When the mixture is thick, add a few drops of lemon and mix again.
  4. Repeat the process until you get the desired amount.
